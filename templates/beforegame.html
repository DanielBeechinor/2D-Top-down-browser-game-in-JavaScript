This is a zombie hoarding game

Your objective it to kill all of the zombies on screen

using the arrow keys to shoot and increasing the round

On the bottom of the screen you will find useful information

such as health, round number, bullet damage and revives

After each round the number of zombies per round will increase and

you will randomly get an item which will be explained in the bottom of the

screen

You use WASD to walk

The zombies get more powerful each round but so do you

The random drops make each game a bit unique so try it out a few times
